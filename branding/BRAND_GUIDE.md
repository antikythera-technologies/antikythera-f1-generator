# Antikythera F1 Brand Guide

## Logo

### Primary Logo
The Antikythera F1 logo features a steampunk-inspired F1 racing helmet with the following key elements:

- **Helmet Shell**: Copper and bronze mechanical gears, cogwheels, and clockwork mechanisms
- **Visor**: Smooth, futuristic blue-teal glass with subtle reflections (NO gears)
- **Border**: Large gear ring behind the helmet
- **Text**: "ANTIKYTHERA" in brushed steel industrial font
- **Badge**: "F1" on a copper plate with patina and rivets
- **Background**: Dark starry/space background

### Logo Files
| File | Usage |
|------|-------|
| `logo/logo-primary.png` | Full logo with text and badge |
| `logo/logo-icon.png` | Helmet only (for favicons, avatars) |
| `logo/logo-dark-bg.png` | Optimized for dark backgrounds |

### Clear Space
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "F1" badge.

### Minimum Size
- **Print**: 1 inch / 25mm width minimum
- **Digital**: 80px width minimum

## Typography

### Headings
- **Font**: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- **Weight**: 600-700 (Semi-bold to Bold)
- **Style**: Uppercase for major headings

### Body Text
- **Font**: Inter, system-ui, sans-serif
- **Weight**: 400-500 (Regular to Medium)
- **Line Height**: 1.5-1.6

### Monospace (Code/Data)
- **Font**: JetBrains Mono, monospace
- **Weight**: 400

---

## Visual Style

### Theme
- **Era**: Steampunk meets futuristic
- **Materials**: Copper, bronze, brushed steel, glass
- **Mood**: Premium, technical, mechanical precision

### Effects
- Subtle glow effects on interactive elements (cyan/teal)
- Metallic gradients for accents
- Dark, space-inspired backgrounds

### Icons
- Use outline style icons
- Copper or teal accent colors
- Consistent stroke width (1.5-2px)

---

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
✅ Use the logo on dark backgrounds  
✅ Maintain aspect ratio when scaling  
✅ Use brand colors consistently  
✅ Apply metallic effects sparingly  

### Don'ts
❌ Don't place logo on busy backgrounds  
❌ Don't stretch or distort the logo  
❌ Don't change the logo colors  
❌ Don't add effects (shadows, outlines) to the logo
